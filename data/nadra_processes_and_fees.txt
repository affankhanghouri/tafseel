[SECTION: SERVICE TIERS OVERVIEW]
================================================================================

NADRA offers THREE processing tiers for all identity documents:

TIER 1 - NORMAL  : Most affordable. Longest processing time. Best for non-urgent needs.
TIER 2 - URGENT  : Moderate fee. Faster processing. Best for upcoming travel/deadlines.
TIER 3 - EXECUTIVE: Highest fee. Fastest processing. Best for emergencies.

IMPORTANT: All processing timelines begin AFTER fee submission and application approval.
Delivery charges are NOT included in any of the fees listed below.
Domestic delivery fee: Rs. 165 (within Pakistan)
International delivery: Varies by country

[SECTION: CNIC — COMPUTERIZED NATIONAL IDENTITY CARD]
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: CNIC — ELIGIBILITY]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ELIGIBILITY: All Pakistani citizens aged 18 years and above.
LEGAL REQUIREMENT: Must apply within ONE MONTH of turning 18.
LEGAL OFFENCE: Not applying within 90 days of turning 18 is an offence under NADRA Ordinance 2000, Section 30(1)(e).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: CNIC — FEE STRUCTURE]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

STANDARD CNIC (Non-Smart):
CATEGORY     | FEE      | PROCESSING TIME
New (Fresh)  | Rs. 0    | 15 working days (Normal)
Modification | Rs. 400  | 15 days Normal / 12 days Urgent / 6 days Executive
Duplicate    | Rs. 400  | 15 days Normal / 12 days Urgent / 6 days Executive
Renewal      | Rs. 400  | 15 days Normal / 12 days Urgent / 6 days Executive
Urgent fee for all categories above: Rs. 1,150
Executive fee for all categories above: Rs. 2,150

SMART CNIC / SNIC (Chip-based):
CATEGORY     | NORMAL (31 days) | URGENT (23 days) | EXECUTIVE (9 days)
New/Fresh    | Rs. 750          | Rs. 1,500        | Rs. 2,500
Modification | Rs. 750          | Rs. 1,500        | Rs. 2,500
Duplicate    | Rs. 750          | Rs. 1,500        | Rs. 2,500
Renewal      | Rs. 750          | Rs. 1,500        | Rs. 2,500

VALIDITY: 10 years for adults. Cards for minors have shorter validity.
LOST/STOLEN CNIC: No additional documents required; card re-issued with existing data.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: CNIC — HOW TO APPLY AT NRC (NADRA REGISTRATION CENTER)]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1 : Visit nearest NRC and obtain a Qmatic token.
STEP 2 : Photograph will be captured.
STEP 3 : Fingerprints and signature taken.
STEP 4 : Data entry completed; digital form displayed for review.
STEP 5 : Biometric verification by a blood relative (Father/Mother/Brother/Sister/Son/Daughter).
         OR attestation by a Gazetted Officer (Grade 16+) if no relative available.
STEP 6 : Interview and approval by Officer-in-Charge (OIC).
STEP 7 : Fee submission.
STEP 8 : CNIC printed and handed over upon completion of processing period.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS FOR FRESH CNIC:
- Birth Certificate (Computerized, issued by UC/Municipal Committee/Cantonment)
  OR Matric/Secondary School Certificate
- CNIC copy of a parent or sibling (for biometric verification)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: CNIC — HOW TO APPLY VIA PAK ID MOBILE APP]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
APP NAVIGATION: Pak ID App > ID Documents > ID Card > Myself / My Blood Relative > Conversion/Renewal/Modification

NOTE: Fresh/New CNIC CANNOT be applied through the Pak ID App or website.
      New applicants must visit a NADRA Registration Center (NRC) in person.

ONLINE AVAILABLE CATEGORIES: Renewal, Modification, Reprint/Duplicate only.

STEP 1 : Log in or create account on Pak ID app (Android/iOS).
STEP 2 : Select ID Card and choose category (Renewal/Modification/Duplicate).
STEP 3 : Capture photograph and fingerprints through the app.
STEP 4 : Enter required data; upload supporting documents if needed.
STEP 5 : Biometric verification by a blood relative through app OR attestation by Gazetted Officer.
STEP 6 : Review and submit application.
STEP 7 : Pay fee (Debit/Credit Card, Raast, Easypaisa, JazzCash, e-Sahulat).
STEP 8 : CNIC will be delivered to your doorstep.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: CNIC — MODIFICATION FIELDS]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
PRINTABLE FIELDS (require documents): Name, Father/Mother Name, Date of Birth,
Address, Photograph, Signature, Marital Status.

NON-PRINTABLE FIELDS (nominal fee Rs. 100 via NRC / $5 via App): Marital Status,
Qualification, Profession, Contact Details.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: CNIC — EXPEDITING AN EXISTING APPLICATION]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
You can upgrade Normal/Urgent to Executive by paying the fee difference:
STEP 1 : Log in to Pak Identity Mobile App.
STEP 2 : Go to NADRA Miscellaneous Services > Application Priority.
STEP 3 : Enter Tracking ID and PIN Code. Press Next.
STEP 4 : Pay the fee difference.
STEP 5 : Receive acknowledgment from NADRA.

[SECTION: NICOP — NATIONAL IDENTITY CARD FOR OVERSEAS PAKISTANIS]
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: NICOP — ELIGIBILITY]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Eligible individuals:
- Pakistani citizens residing abroad who hold nationality of a dual-nationality country
- Citizens who are emigrants or intending emigrants (with foreign employment agreement)
- Citizens holding foreign residence visa/permit
- Dependents of emigrants

DUAL NATIONALITY COUNTRIES: Australia, Belgium, Canada, Egypt, France, Iceland,
Ireland, Italy, Jordan, Netherlands (Holland), New Zealand, Sweden, Switzerland,
Syria, UK, USA, Bahrain, Finland, Denmark, Germany, Norway, Luxembourg.

BENEFIT: NICOP holders can enter Pakistan without a visa on a foreign passport.

SMART NICOP ELIGIBILITY DOCUMENTS — UNDER 18 YEARS:

- Pakistani/Foreign passport OR residence permit OR travel document
  OR Undertaking "A" attested by Pakistani embassy/mission

SMART NICOP ELIGIBILITY DOCUMENTS — 18 YEARS AND ABOVE:
- Identity document of parents or sibling
- Foreign detailed Birth Certificate OR manual Birth Certificate OR S-1 Form
  OR Computerized Birth Certificate (verified)
- Pakistani/Foreign passport OR residence permit OR travel document
  OR Undertaking "A" attested by Pakistani embassy/mission

ALREADY REGISTERED: If you already have a CRC, Juvenile Card, or CNIC — provide that number.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: NICOP — IMPORTANT RULES]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Foreign address can only be added in ONE address field (Present OR Permanent). The other must be a Pakistan address.
- Fingerprints/signatures NOT required for children under 15 years of age.
- For Smart NICOP attestation: mandatory step even for applications made abroad.

[SECTION: POC — PAKISTAN ORIGIN CARD]
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: POC — ELIGIBILITY]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Issued to eligible FOREIGNERS of Pakistani origin:
1. Person who was a Pakistani citizen at any point in life.
2. Individual whose any parent or grandparent (alive or deceased) was a Pakistani citizen.
3. Individual (or their parents/grandparents) born in territory now included in Pakistan.
4. Individual who acquired foreign nationality before Pakistan Citizenship Act 1951.
5. Spouse of a Pakistani citizen or person of Pakistani origin.
6. Dependent child of a foreign spouse.

ALSO ELIGIBLE:
- Foreign national married to a Pakistani citizen whose parents are of Pakistani origin.
- Foreign national whose parents/grandparents are/were Pakistani nationals.
- Foreign national whose relatives (brother/sister/uncle/aunt) are/were Pakistani nationals.

NOT ELIGIBLE (Foreign Spouse category): Citizens of India, Israel, Taiwan, or enemy countries.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: POC — FEE STRUCTURE (in USD)]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CATEGORY   | NORMAL (31 days) | URGENT (23 days) | EXECUTIVE (9 days)
New        | $150             | $200             | $250
Renewal    | $150             | $200             | $250
Modification| $200            | $250             | $300
Duplicate  | $200             | $250             | $300

VALIDITY:
- POC based on ex-Pakistani/Parents/FPO family: 7 years
- POC based on Spouse: 5 years

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: POC — HOW TO APPLY]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
APPLICATION METHOD: Exclusively through Pak ID Mobile App. Delivered to doorstep.
CATEGORIES: New / Modification / Renewal / Cancellation

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: POC — DATA MODIFICATION FIELDS AND REQUIRED DOCUMENTS]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

DATE OF BIRTH CHANGE:
- Original POC
- Valid foreign passport with new date of birth

NAME CHANGE:
- Original POC
- Valid foreign passport with new name
- Name change deed (translated to English)

PERMANENT PAKISTANI ADDRESS:
- Scanned copy of POC
- NICOP/POC of blood relatives/spouse with same address OR
- Property document OR Tenancy agreement OR Relative's affidavit

MARITAL STATUS:
- Original POC and valid foreign passport
- Marriage: Nikahnama OR Marriage Certificate
- Re-marriage after death of spouse: Death Certificate + Latest Marriage Certificate
- Re-marriage after divorce: Divorce Certificate + Latest Marriage Certificate
- Widowed: Death Certificate of spouse + Affidavit

NOTE: Foreign spouse POC holders cannot modify from Married to Divorced.

[SECTION: CRC — CHILD REGISTRATION CERTIFICATE (B-FORM)]
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: CRC — ELIGIBILITY]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Every Pakistani child UNDER 18 years of age whose parent(s) hold a valid CNIC/NICOP.
LEGAL REQUIREMENT: Parents must register child within ONE MONTH of birth.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: CRC — FEE STRUCTURE]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CATEGORY           | FEE      | PROCESSING TIME
Normal             | Rs. 50   | 7 days
Executive          | Rs. 500  | 1 day

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: CRC — REQUIRED DOCUMENTS]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
INLAND BIRTHS  : Computerized Birth Registration Certificate (from UC/Municipal Committee/Cantonment)
ABROAD BIRTHS  : Detailed Birth Certificate OR S-1 Form
PARENT REQUIREMENT: Parent(s) must have valid CNIC or NICOP

PHOTOGRAPH AND FINGERPRINT REQUIREMENTS:
- Children up to 3 years     : Present only if CRC is for passport issuance
- Children aged 3 to 10 years: Photograph is MANDATORY
- Children aged 10 to 18 years: Photograph AND Fingerprints are MANDATORY

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: CRC — HOW TO APPLY AT NRC]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1 : Visit nearest NRC; obtain token.
STEP 2 : Biometric verification of parent.
STEP 3 : Photograph and/or fingerprints of child (where required).
STEP 4 : Data entered and reviewed.
STEP 5 : Attestation by Spouse / Guardian / Gazetted Officer.
STEP 6 : Fee submission (Executive Rs. 500 - 1 day / Normal Rs. 50 - 7 days).
STEP 7 : CRC printed and handed over upon completion.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: CRC — HOW TO APPLY VIA PAK ID APP]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
APP NAVIGATION: Pak ID App > Family > CRC or B-Form > Myself / My Blood Relative

STEP 1 : Log in or create Pak ID account.
STEP 2 : Go to Family > CRC or B-Form.
STEP 3 : Choose Myself or My Blood Relatives.
STEP 4 : Select category (Executive Rs. 500 - 1 day / Normal Rs. 50 - 7 days).
STEP 5 : Start Application; review prefilled information.
STEP 6 : Verify parent's details.
STEP 7 : Enter disability details if required.
STEP 8 : Capture child's photograph and fingerprints (where required).
STEP 9 : Verify fingerprints of one parent.
STEP 10: Upload required documents.
STEP 11: Review and submit application.
STEP 12: Pay fee after approval.
STEP 13: CRC delivered as PDF in Pak ID Vault (within 1 day for Executive / 7 days for Normal).

[SECTION: JUVENILE CARD]
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: JUVENILE CARD — OVERVIEW]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Issued to Pakistani children UNDER 18 as an official identity document (physical card, unlike B-Form which is a certificate).
Can be applied at NRC or online via Pak ID App.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: JUVENILE CARD — FEE STRUCTURE]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CATEGORY                  | FEE           | PROCESSING TIME
Executive (excluding delivery) | Rs. 2,500 | 9 days
Urgent (excluding delivery)    | Rs. 1,500 | 23 days
Normal (excluding delivery)    | Rs. 750   | 31 days

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: JUVENILE CARD — REQUIRED DOCUMENTS]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- If child already holds CRC: NO additional documents required.
- If no CRC:
  INLAND BIRTHS  : Computerized Birth Registration Certificate (UC/Municipal/Cantonment)
  ABROAD BIRTHS  : Detailed Birth Certificate OR S-1 Form
- Parent must hold valid CNIC or NICOP.
- Child MUST be physically present.
- Biometric verification of ONE parent with valid CNIC is MANDATORY.

SPECIAL CASE: If both parents are unavailable/deceased:
- Guardian can process Juvenile Card by providing Guardianship Certificate from Guardian Court.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: JUVENILE CARD — HOW TO APPLY AT NRC]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1 : Visit nearest NRC; obtain token.
STEP 2 : Biometric verification of parent or sibling.
STEP 3 : Photographs and fingerprints of child (where required).
STEP 4 : Data entered and reviewed.
STEP 5 : Attestation by one of the parents.
STEP 6 : Interview of parent by OIC.
STEP 7 : Fee submission.
STEP 8 : Juvenile Card printed and handed over after processing period.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: JUVENILE CARD — HOW TO APPLY VIA PAK ID APP]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
APP NAVIGATION: Pak ID App > ID Documents > ID Card > My Blood Relatives

STEP 1 : Log in or create Pak ID account.
STEP 2 : Go to ID Documents > ID Card > My Blood Relatives.
STEP 3 : Enter B-Form number (if available) and select New Smart ID.
STEP 4 : Capture parent's biometrics (verified).
STEP 5 : Review prefilled information; add missing info.
STEP 6 : Enter child's details and parent's details.
STEP 7 : Enter disability details if required.
STEP 8 : Capture child's photo and fingerprints (where required).
STEP 9 : Upload required documents.
STEP 10: Review and submit application.
STEP 11: Pay fee after approval (Executive Rs. 2,500 / Urgent Rs. 1,500 / Normal Rs. 750).
STEP 12: Juvenile Card dispatched to doorstep after processing period.

[SECTION: FRC — FAMILY REGISTRATION CERTIFICATE]
================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: FRC — OVERVIEW]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
Official document reflecting the family composition data of a registered person.
Used for: Embassy applications, visa processing, inheritance, legal/official purposes.
VALIDITY: Valid as proof of family data on the date of issuance.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: FRC — CATEGORIES AND FEES]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
CATEGORY    | DESCRIPTION                                      | FEE
By Birth    | Includes details of parents and siblings         | Rs. 1,000
By Marriage | Includes details of spouse and children          | Rs. 1,000
By Adoption | Includes details of guardian and adopted family  | Rs. 1,000
By All      | Includes both alpha and beta family members      | Rs. 2,000

PROCESSING TIME: Issued in real-time at NRC. Via Pak ID App: PDF within 24 hours.

REQUIRED: 13-digit ID number of applicant (CRC/CNIC/NICOP/POC).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: FRC — HOW TO APPLY AT NRC]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
STEP 1 : Visit nearest NRC; obtain token.
STEP 2 : Biometric verification.
STEP 3 : Photographs of minors taken (if CRC issued without photo).
STEP 4 : Data downloaded and reviewed.
STEP 5 : Fee submission.
STEP 6 : FRC printed and handed over in REAL TIME.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: FRC — HOW TO APPLY VIA PAK ID APP]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
APP NAVIGATION: Pak ID App > Family > FRC

STEP 1 : Log in or create Pak ID account.
STEP 2 : Go to Family > FRC.
STEP 3 : Family member details will appear.
STEP 4 : Records in RED require correction before proceeding.
STEP 5 : Select category: By Birth / By Marriage / By Adoption / By All.
STEP 6 : Review and submit application.
STEP 7 : Pay fee.
STEP 8 : FRC downloaded as PDF in ID Vault within 24 hours.

IMPORTANT NOTES:
- If child under 18 has CRC without photo, child must be physically present at NRC.
- Incomplete records will not appear in FRC; update first, then apply.
- If deceased family member, their CNIC must be cancelled (with Death Certificate) before FRC processing.
- Only English name spelling corrections allowed during FRC; name changes NOT allowed.

================================================================================

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: CANCELLATION — DUE TO DEATH]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FEE: FREE (No charge)
PROCESSING TIME: 24 hours

REQUIRED DOCUMENT:

  OR Foreign Death Certificate

LEGAL NOTE: Family must apply for cancellation within 60 DAYS of death.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: CANCELLATION — DUE TO SURRENDER OF PAKISTANI NATIONALITY]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
FEE: Included in processing (applies for individuals moving to single-nationality countries)
PROCESSING TIME: 2 days

  OR Renunciation Certificate
  OR Dual National Country Passport AND Renunciation Certificate (verified)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
For Ex-Pakistanis resuming Pakistani nationality or when dual nationality agreement enacted.

REQUIRED DOCUMENTS:
- Resumption letter / Citizenship certificate from IMPASS (verified)
  OR Undertaking "I" from applicant (if Pakistani citizenship was not formally renounced)

[TOPIC: CANCELLATION — POC DUE TO DIVORCE (FOREIGN SPOUSE)]

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

[SECTION: MACHINE READABLE PASSPORT (MRP) — OVERVIEW]
================================================================================

NOTE: Passports are issued by the Directorate General of Immigration & Passports (DGIP),
NOT directly by NADRA. However, NADRA records (CNIC/NICOP/CRC) are mandatory for
passport applications. Passport biometrics can now be verified via NADRA PAKID Mobile App.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: PASSPORT — FEE STRUCTURE (INLAND, INCLUDING SERVICE CHARGES)]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------

5-YEAR VALIDITY:
PAGES   | NORMAL      | URGENT
36 pages| Rs. 5,500   | Rs. 8,500
72 pages| Rs. 9,200   | Rs. 14,500
100 pages| Rs. 10,000 | Rs. 19,000

10-YEAR VALIDITY (adults only; minors under 15 are NOT eligible for 10-year passport):
PAGES   | NORMAL      | URGENT
36 pages| Rs. 7,700   | Rs. 12,200
72 pages| Rs. 13,400  | Rs. 21,200
100 pages| Rs. 14,500 | Rs. 28,000

PROCESSING TIMES:
Normal: Up to 21 working days
Urgent: ~5 working days
Fast-Track (EPO): ~2 working days (available at Executive Passport Offices in Islamabad/Lahore/Karachi)

LOST PASSPORT REPLACEMENT:
First loss: Higher than standard renewal (approx. Rs. 9,000 for 36-page normal MRP)
Second loss: Significantly higher
Third loss or more: Highest fee (deterrent pricing)
NOTE: Lost passport replacement requires a Police Loss Report mentioning previous passport number.

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: PASSPORT — REQUIRED DOCUMENTS (ADULTS, 18+)]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Original valid CNIC/NICOP with photocopy
- Previous passport (original + photocopy) if applicable
- Bank paid fee challan OR e-payment confirmation (PSID No.)
- NOC from department (for government/semi-government employees)
- Foreign passport + photocopy (for dual nationals only)
- Police report + previous passport number (for lost passports)

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: PASSPORT — REQUIRED DOCUMENTS (MINORS, UNDER 18)]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Original CRC (updated with photo and fingerprints for children 10+) OR Juvenile Card
- OR NICOP for overseas minors
- CNICs/Passports of both parents
- Birth Certificate (with both parents' names)
- Minor must be accompanied by one or both parents
- For divorced/separated parents: Court order confirming custody

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: PASSPORT — HOW TO APPLY ONLINE (RENEWAL ONLY)]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
ONLINE PORTAL: https://onlinemrp.dgip.gov.pk

NOTE: Online service is for RENEWAL only (inland applicants).
New, Modification, Lost, and Damaged passports require in-person visit.
Overseas Pakistanis can apply online for Renewal, New (newborns up to 2 years), Reprint, Damaged, Lost, and Modification.

STEPS:
1. Register/login on online portal.
2. Select Renewal category.
3. Upload photograph and fingerprints (600 DPI, JPG, Grayscale).
4. Upload supporting documents.
5. Pay fee online (Visa/Mastercard, e-Sahulat, Easypaisa, JazzCash).
6. Track application via SMS to 9988 (send 11-digit token number).
7. Collect passport from preferred RPO/EPO (inland) OR home delivery (overseas).

FINGERPRINT VERIFICATION: Can now be done via NADRA PAKID Mobile App (replaces manual form).

--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
[TOPIC: PASSPORT — IMPORTANT RULES]
--------------------------------------------------------------------------------
- Passport MUST be renewed if expiring within 6 months (mandatory for travel).
- For data changes (name, DOB, etc.), first get CNIC/NICOP modified, THEN apply for passport.
- Fees are NON-REFUNDABLE, even if application is rejected.
- Fee is the same for renewal and new applications (standard rates apply).
- Passport fee payment methods: Passport Fee Asaan App, bank PSID payment, e-Sahulat.
- Children under 15 can only get 5-year validity passports.
- Fast-Track service NOT available for e-Passport delivery.
